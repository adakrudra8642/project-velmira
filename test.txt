# Project Chimera: Evolutionary Robotics Simulator

Chimera is a lightweight evolutionary engine that pairs a 3D physics simulator (PyBullet) with a genetic algorithm to co-design a robot's body and brain for a given locomotion task.

## Technical Specifications
- Simulation: PyBullet 3.25+
- Genetic Algorithm: (μ+λ) with μ=20, λ=100
- Mutation rate: 0.15 per gene
- Crossover: uniform with 0.7 probability
- Fitness: distance traveled in 5 seconds minus energy cost

## Hardware Requirements
- CPU: Intel Core i5 or equivalent (8+ threads recommended)
- RAM: 16 GB minimum (32 GB for complex morphologies)
- GPU: None required (CPU-only simulation)

## Known Limitations
- Maximum 12 actuated joints per robot (performance constraint)
- No soft body physics yet
- Fitness evaluation takes ~15 seconds per genome on reference hardware
